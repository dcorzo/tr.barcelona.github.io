Meet Barcelona's Best Youngsters

8 Pedri

Age: 20
Position: Central Midfield
Market Value: € 100.00M

Pedri has been an excellent midfielder for Barcelona. Through his intelligence and creativity, he has gone up the ranks and proven to the world that he is one of the current best youngsters.

6 Gavi

Age: 19
Position: Central Midfield
Market Value: € 90.00M

Gavi made his name known through his aggressive play style, reinvigorating the midfield with the toughness once commonly practiced in older football days. His passion radiates whenever he steps onto the field.

3 Alejandro Balde

Age: 19
Position: Left-Back
Market Value: € 50.00M

Alejandro Balde had recently sprouted at Barcelona but is already recognized as one of the best young left-backs in the world. Through his fast pace and dribbling skills, he has continuously dominated the left-flank.
